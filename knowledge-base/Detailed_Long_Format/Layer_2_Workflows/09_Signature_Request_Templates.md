# Signature Request Templates

Signature Request Templates let your organization encode complex business rules into reusable workflow blueprints, ensuring every contract type is dispatched with the correct authentication requirements, signing order, compliance settings, and branding - regardless of which team member sends it.

- **What a template pre-configures** - authentication method and strength (Strong AES or Light SES, separately for reading and signing), delivery channel (email, SMS, or both), signing order (simultaneous or sequential with signatory groups), reminder schedule, mandatory reflection periods, branded PAdES cover page with your company logo, SSN collection and hashing rules, signatory forwarding permissions, watcher/follower rules, signer document upload requirements, and expiry period

- **Enforcing process consistency** - templates remove human decision-making from compliance-critical settings. Instead of relying on individual employees to remember the right authentication level or waiting period, the template hardcodes these rules. Settings can be locked (greyed out in the portal) so users cannot override them, while other fields remain editable for flexibility

- **Multiple templates for different processes** - organizations typically maintain separate templates per contract type. A bank loan template enforces a 7-14 day reflection period, strong authentication, and a branded cover page with signatory roles (Applicant, Guarantor). An HR onboarding template enables sequential signing (candidate then manager), activates signer document upload for ID copies, and uses a different branded layout. A supplier NDA template allows simultaneous signing with standard settings

- **Template vs. from scratch** - when creating from a template, the portal pre-fills all workflow rules from the dropdown selection; the sender only adds recipients and uploads the document. Creating from scratch requires the sender to manually configure every setting, increasing the risk of errors on compliance-sensitive contracts
