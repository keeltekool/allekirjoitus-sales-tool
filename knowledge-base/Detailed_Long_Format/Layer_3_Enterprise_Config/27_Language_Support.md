# Language Support

Your signing workflows operate in the language your signers understand, covering every element from portal navigation to the notification emails that land in their inbox and the signing interface where they execute the agreement.

- **Available languages** - the platform supports Finnish, Swedish, English, Danish, and Norwegian. When creating a standard signature request in the portal, the sender selects from the primary language options via a dropdown menu. Enterprise customers using Online Forms can build localized form versions across supported languages at no additional fee

- **Where language applies** - language localization covers the entire journey: the sender's portal UI, the signer-facing signing flow pages (instructions, buttons, prompts, document summary views), all notification emails, and SMS messages. Tenant-level message templates can be configured with different language variants, so your invitation and reminder texts are properly localized for each market

- **How language is selected** - the sender chooses the language per request during the first step of creating a new signing request, immediately after entering the subject line. This selection determines the language of all signer-facing elements for that specific transaction. A tenant-level default can be set so that your most common language is pre-selected, reducing repetitive configuration for senders who primarily work in one market

- **What the signer experiences** - the signer receives notification emails in the selected language. When they click the signing link, every element of the signing interface - instructions, action buttons, status messages, and prompts - is presented in that language. This ensures your signers fully understand the actions they are taking, reducing confusion and drop-off during the signing ceremony, particularly important when your counterparties span multiple Nordic markets
