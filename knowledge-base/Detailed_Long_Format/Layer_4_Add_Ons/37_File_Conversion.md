# File Conversion

When your team works with Office documents or image files, the platform automatically converts them to archival-grade PDF/A format upon upload - so your senders can attach what they have without worrying about format requirements.

- **What gets converted** - Microsoft Office files (Word, Excel) and common image formats are supported. The platform converts them into PDF/A, the ISO-standardized PDF format designed specifically for long-term preservation and electronic archiving

- **When conversion happens** - the conversion is fully automated and occurs immediately after the file is uploaded to the system, before the signing workflow begins. There is no manual conversion step and no separate tool required

- **What PDF/A means and why it matters** - PDF/A embeds all necessary fonts, colors, and structural elements directly within the file, guaranteeing that the document can be opened, read, and verified years or decades later exactly as it appeared on the day it was signed. This is critical for legal and compliance workflows where archived documents must remain readable and verifiable in external archiving systems long after signing

- **Transparent to the sender** - the process is frictionless. Your team simply uploads the raw Office or image file as they normally would. The system converts it in the background, and the sender can visually review the converted PDF directly in the portal before dispatching the signature request. There is no format rejection, no error message, and no need to open a separate application to save as PDF first

- **Enterprise add-on** - File Conversion is available as a transactional add-on for Enterprise customers, billed per converted document
