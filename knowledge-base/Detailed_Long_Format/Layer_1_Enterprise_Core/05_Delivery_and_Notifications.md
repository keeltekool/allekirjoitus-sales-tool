# Delivery & Notifications

Signing requests reach your signers via email or SMS, and all parties stay informed with automated notifications throughout the signing lifecycle. Enterprise customers get full control over branding, timing, channels, and whether the platform handles notifications at all.

- **Email and SMS delivery** - email is the standard delivery channel for all customers, sending a secure signing link to each signer's inbox. Enterprise customers can additionally dispatch invitations via SMS, sending the link directly to the signer's phone. SMS delivery is billed separately per message. You can use email, SMS, or both per recipient on the same request

- **Notification language** - the sender selects the language per request from Finnish, Swedish, or English during the first step of creating a new signing request. This selection determines the language of all signer-facing elements: invitation emails, reminder messages, the signing interface instructions and buttons, and completion notifications

- **Automatic reminders** - the platform sends reminder emails on a default schedule: one reminder two days after dispatch, and a final warning one day before the request expires. The reminder contains the same secure signing link as the original invitation. Enterprise customers can fully customize these intervals, timing reminders forward from dispatch or backward from expiry. Reminders can be sent via email or SMS

- **Manual reminders** - senders can trigger on-demand ad-hoc reminders to individual signers directly from the portal dashboard. This is also used after correcting a bounced email address to immediately resend the invitation. Manual reminder capability is Enterprise-only

- **Completion and OTP-protected download** - once all required parties sign, every participant receives a completion notification email. To download the finalized signed PDF, the signer must verify their identity through a one-time password sent via SMS, adding a security layer to document retrieval

- **Enterprise branding and suppression** - customize notification email templates with your logo, colors, and legal disclaimers. Configure a custom sender domain (default pattern: noreply+TenantName@teliasign.com, or fully custom with DNS verification). For API-driven workflows where your own system handles communications, suppress platform notifications entirely per Signature Request Template
